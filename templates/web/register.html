Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for courses?
To register for courses, log in to your student portal or the institution's online platform. Navigate to the section for course registration, select the courses you wish to take, and add them to your schedule.
What should I do if a course I want is full?
If a course you're interested in is full, check if there's a waitlist option available. If so, join the waitlist.
Can I make changes to my course schedule
Yes, you can usually make changes to your course schedule within a specified add/drop period. This period varies by institution and is typically mentioned in the academic calendar.
